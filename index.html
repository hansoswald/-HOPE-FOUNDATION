Our Mission

To empower Children, Youths and Women from vulnerable communities through Quality Education, WASH services, Sexual Reproductive and Sexual Rights (SRHR), Climate Action and Economic opportunities, thus achieving Equitable and poverty free, self-reliant lives and community resilience.

Our Vision

Envisions a sustainable planet where Social, economic and Environmental inclusion to Children, Youths and women becomes a reality for attaining self-reliant, poverty free lives for better future.

Our Objectives

  1. Promoting equitable and quality education
  2. Enhancing economic and financial opportunities
  3. Promoting Environmental Sustainability
  4. Providing access to safe Water and adequate sanitation and hygiene services in Schools and communities
  5. Advocating for Sexual and Reproductive and health rights (SHRHR) in the community

Our Approaches

Grassroot Solutions: Full participation of the community, working with local authority leaders, women and youth groups.

Skills Development: Life skills, GBV, financial literacy, entrepreneurship, WASH skills and climate action.

Access to Resources: Safe water, education, startup capital, markets and other economic tools.

Gender Equality and Inclusion: Equal access to services for all, inclusive participation, breaking bias and achieving gender justice.

Our Impact

About Us

Their Hope Foundation (T.H.F) is a Women and Youth-led NGO registered under the NGO Act No.24 of 2002 of Tanzania (Reg No: ooNGO/R/8246) based in Dar es Salaam. We empower Children, Youths and Women in vulnerable communities with Education, WASH, SRHR, Climate Action, and Economic Opportunities.

Our Core Values

  • Diversity and Inclusion: Everyone feels valued and respected.
  • Integrity: We act honestly and transparently.
  • Partnership: We collaborate with NGOs, private sector, and government.
  • Innovation: We encourage creative solutions.
  • Community Driven: Community-led planning and implementation.
